INTRO

Welcome to the Global Research Center for Quantum Information Science

Our research center is dedicated to advancing quantum information science by exploring fundamental principles at the intersection of quantum physics and information theory. Over the past few decades, quantum technologies have experienced remarkable progress. Although many engineering challenges still demand practical solutions, continuous theoretical inquiry remains equally essential to drive sustained advancements.

Join us as we shape the future of quantum information technologies and explore their profound implications for science, industry, society, and our future.

As a member of Japan's Inter-University Research Institute Corporations, we actively support an international community of scholars, students, and industry partners through collaborative research opportunities and extensive academic resources.
